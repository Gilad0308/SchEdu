Welcome to SchEdu – your smart Education Scheduler!

Quickly and easily create perfect class schedules for your school needs.
Simply define your schedule framework, study data & classes, and let SchEdu’s smart algorithm take care of the rest.
Just click the ‘Get started’ button and follow the instructions.

Get Started >>

To begin your SchEdu configuration, follow these three simple steps:
1. Framework – define your ‘when & where’ including available study days, class hours & classrooms.
2. Study Data – provide the data required to populate the study framework including desired subjects, corresponding teachers & study groups.
3. Classes – aggregate your study data into classes.
Then, go to 'Schedules', click 'Create' and let the SchEdu smart algorithm build your perfect schedule.
